  • Integrated Child Development Scheme (ICDS) was started by which Prime Minister
Indira Gandhi
  • ICDS was started during which Five year plan
5th Five year plan
  • ICDS was lunched on
2nd October 1975 (106th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi)
  • ICDS comes under
Ministry of women and child development
  • Coordination of ICDS in block level is by
Child development project officer
  • Coordination of ICDS in district level is by
District Program Officer
  • Main services sponsored under ICDS are
Immunization, Supplementary nutrition, Health checkup, Pre-school non-formal education, nutrition and health information, Referral services
  • The institution provides credit for ICDS projects like construction of Anganvadi buildings
World bank
  • Main beneficiaries of ICDS
Children belongs to 0-6 years of age, Pregnant and lactating mothers and adolescent girls in backward rural areas, tribal areas and urban areas
  • For nutritional purpose of children below 6 years of age ICDS provide
300 Calories with 8-10 gms of protein everyday
  • For nutritional purpose of adolescent girls ICDS provide
500 Calories with 25 gms of protein everyday
  • Activities of ICDS are conducted through
Anganvadies
  • International organizations providing assistance to ICDS
World bank and UNICEF
  • Sneha Shivir is the component of
ICDS
  • First ICDS project in Kerala was setup in 1975 at
Vengara Block, Malappuram
  • Primary target of ICDS was
Reduce the child death ration to 2/3 by 2015

7th Five year plan

Additional gainful employment for the unemployed and under employed person in rural areas

JRY
  • Minimum number of days of employment provided by JRY
90-100 Days
  • Percentage of opportunities shared for women in JRY
30%
  • Who is responsible for the work planning and implementation of JRY
Grama Panchayats
  • Plan which is considered as successor of JRY
Jawahar Gram Samridhi Yojana (JGSY)
  • JGSY was introduced in
1st April 1999
  • JGSY was merged with Sampoorna Grameen Rozgar Yojana (SGRY) on
25th September 2001

Neighbourhood group

Empowerment of women through self help groups and encouraging entrepreneurial or other wide range of activicties

Local self government
  • Funds for Kudumbashree channelized through
Local self government

Ward level of Panchayat/Municipality

Micro credit, entrepreneurship and empowerment
