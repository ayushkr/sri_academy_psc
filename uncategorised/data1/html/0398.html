23rd July 2010

18 B B village in Sriganga district of Rajastan

Provide connectivity to all the eligible unconnected habitations of more than 500 persons in the rural areas (250 in hilly and desert areas) by good quality all weather roads, development of villages having a higher ratio (over 50%) of people belonging to Scheduled Casts, eliminate disparity between SC and other communities

Build an Adarsh gram with adequate physical and institutional infrastructure to meet all the sections of the society

Bharat Nirman, PMGSY, SSA, MGNREGA and ICDS
