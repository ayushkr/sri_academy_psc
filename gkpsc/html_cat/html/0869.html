    The Department of Posts released a special cover on ‘Buddha Purnima’:

  21. The Department of Posts released a special cover on ‘Buddha Purnima’ in Chennai recently. The cover was released by J.T. Venkateswarulu, Postmaster General (Mail and Business Development).

  22. Centre to fund inland waterways:

  23. The Central government has completed a feasibility study on introduction of inland waterways on the Godavari over 1,184 kms from Nashik to Bhadrachalam and ready to take it up this year by funding Rs. 2,000 crore.
  24. It has invited Telangana, Andhra Pradesh and Maharashtra which are the riparian States of Godavari to have a 10 per cent stake each in a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) to be set up for the purpose.

  25. CURRENT AFFAIRS: BANKING AND FINANCE

    RBI to banks: Share foreign exchange data with DRI:

  26. Reserve Bank of India has directed banks authorised to deal in foreign exchange (Authorised Dealer-I Banks) to share data with the Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI).
  27. This decision has been made in line with the government making rules (in December 2017) to exercise powers conferred by the relevant sections of the Customs Act.
  28. This requires a banking company to furnish, electronically, information relating to foreign exchange transactions made or received by any person to the receiving authority (DRI).
  29. DRI is the top intelligence and investigative agency for issues related to violation of the Customs Act.

  30. RBI announced Rs.10,000 crore bond purchases:

  31. Reserve Bank of India (RBI) announced that, it will buy Rs 10,000 crore of government bonds through open market operation (OMO).
  32. RBI made this decision after failing to sell all the debt it had offered to bidders. It was the third consecutive week when the RBI couldn’t sell everything on offer which lead to a spike in bond yields.
  33. RBI said that, the decision is based on an assessment of prevailing liquidity conditions and also of the durable liquidity needs going forward.
