Current Affairs Short Updates – 02nd May 2018

  1. Government is committed to empower farmers and double their income by 2022.
  2. Cabinet approves new terminal buildings at Guwahati, Chennai and Lucknow airports.
  3. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has allowed foreign portfolio investors (FPIs) to invest in treasury bills issued by the central government.
  4. GAIL signs Rs 2,000-cr loan agreement with SBI.
  5. Government enters the agreement with World Bank on biotech.
  6. Subhash Chandra Khuntia is new IRDAI chairman.
  7. Shri Sitanshu Kar takes over as DG, PIB.
  8. 9th India-Japan Energy Dialogue held in New Delhi.
  9. Bilateral trade between India and China resumes through Nathu La border.
  10. Asha Bhosle Honored with PC Chandra Puraskar.
  11. South Western Command’s Exercise Vijay Prahar.
  12. India’s Neeraj Goyat is WBC Asia Boxer of the Year.
  13. Hockey India names Harendra Singh as men’s coach.
  14. Wimbledon increases prize money.
  15. Eminent Economist Ashok Mitra Passes Away.
