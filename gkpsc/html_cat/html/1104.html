Aadhar enabled e-PAN card service launched by Income Tax Department
On July 2, 2018, The Income Tax Department has launched instant Aadhaar-based e-PAN allotment service for individuals seeking PAN (Permanent Account Number) service for the first time.
Eligibility:
i. The pan service is only available for valid Aadhar card holders.
ii. Eligible only the only for resident individuals and not for Hindu Undivided Family (HUF), firms, trusts and companies.
About the e-PAN service:
i. This facility is free of cost and is available for a limited period on first-come, first-served basis.
ii. The e-PAN will be allotted on basis of one-time password (OTP) sent on mobile number linked to Aadhaar number of a person.
iii. It will have the same name, date of birth, gender, mobile number and address as in Aadhaar card.
iv. Once e-PAN is allotted to applicant through his electronic Aadhaar-based verification system in few seconds, the applicant will be sent the PAN card by post.
v. This process is done via the official website of the IT dept
About PAN Card:
♦ PAN is a ten-digit alphanumeric number, issued by the Income Tax Department of India in form of a laminated tamper proof card. It was introduced to facilitates linking of various documents, including payment of taxes, assessment, tax demand, tax arrears etc for tax detection and combating tax evasion.
♦ It is unique to each and every individual or entity and is valid across India.

Ravindra Dholakia committee: Government forms panel to upgrade norms for state, district level economic data collection
On July 1,2018, The government has set up a 13-member committee for Sub-National Accounts will be headed by Ravindra H Dholakia, a retired professor of IIM Ahmedabad.
Objective:
To upgrade the norms for computation of economic data at states and districts level to revise the base year for National Accounts or GDP calculation.
Key Points:
i. The Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MOSPI) will change the base year to 2017-18 for the calculation of GDP and IIP numbers from the current 2011-12 with an aim to capture changes in the economy.
ii. It would review the concepts, definitions, classifications, data conventions, data sources and data requirements for preparation of State Domestic Product (SDP) and District Domestic Product (DDP).
iii. It will also suggest measures for improving SDP and DDP in the country, suggest state-level annual/benchmark surveys.
iv. It was suggested that same principles and concepts should be used while calculating SDP and DDP across the country to make data comparable.
v. The panel would submit its report within one year.
About Central Statictics Office (CSO):
The Central Statistics Office (CSO), MOSOPI, revises the base year of the macroeconomic indicators, as a regular exercise, to capture structural changes in the economy and improve the quality and representativeness of the indices. The CSO had last updated base year for GDP calculation to 2011-12 from January 2015, replacing the old series base year of 2004-05

LIC gets IRDAI’s nod to buy stake in IDBI Bank
On June 30,2018, The Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) approved Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) of India’s proposal to hike its stake to 51 per cent in IDBI Bank from 10.8 per cent.
i. LIC is expected to infuse Rs 13,000 crore into IDBI Bank.
About the Deal:
i. An additional 40.2 per cent stake for IDBI Bank will cost LIC Rs 9,229 crore at the current share price of nearly Rs 55.
ii. According to BSE, currently the government holds 81% of the IDBI Bank.
iii. LIC-IDBI Bank deal will lead to a series of exemptions to be given by three regulators — Irdai, the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi) and the Reserve Bank of India (RBI).
iv. LIC would be exempted from the provisions in the Insurance Laws (Amendment) Act, 2015, and the Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956, that bar insurers from holding more than 15% in any company.
v. Thus,the permission is subject to the corporation bringing down its holding to 15 per cent in future.
vi. This acquisition is expected to boost business through the bancassurance channel.
Reasons for the deal:
i. In the Budget for 2016-17, the government had said it could consider trimming its stake in IDBI Bank below 50% but the government cannot bring down its share below 51% without amending Banking regulation Act 1949.
ii. The deal will save the government from having to infuse more funds into IDBI Bank, which had received as much as Rs 10,610 crore last fiscal, the most by any public-sector bank (PSB).
Other deals of LIC:
LIC had picked up a 28 per cent strategic stake in Corporation Bank, which is now currently down to 13 per cent

First stainless steel bodied Mainline Electric Multiple Unit(MEMU) launched by Integral Coach Factory
On June 30,2018, The first three phase energy efficient Mainline Electric Multiple Unit (MEMU) coaches built with stainless steel bodies were rolled out by Integral Coach Factory (ICF) in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
i. The train was flagged off by Senior Section Engineer Saseedharan in the presence of former railway board officials B Dasarathy and Vijaya Kanth.
ii. ICF General Manager S Mani was also present in the inauguration.
About the MEMU :
i. The MEMU train consists of eight coaches and two driving motor coaches.
ii. The carrying capacity of trailer coaches is 325 with 84 seats, while the motor coaches which has 55 seats can carry upto 226 passengers.
iii. Thus, the rake can carry about 2,402 passengers per trip.
iv. The first rake is to be sent to Southern East Central Railway.
Benefits provided in the MEMU train:
i. The MEMU rake has modular toilets in every coach.
ii. It will run on self-propelled 25 kv AC Overhead Traction, supported by Regenerative braking system.
iii. It will save energy consumed up to 35 per cent compared to conventional EMU and MEMU trains.
iv. The coaches have been provided with CCTV for enhanced passenger safety and GPS based Passenger Information System (PIS) and Passenger Address System (PAS) also have been provided in all coaches.
v. Its maximum speed is 110kmph.
About Integral Coach Factory(ICF):
Integral Coach Factory is a manufacturer of rail coaches located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It was established in 1952, is owned and operated by the Indian Railways.

Successful launch of SES-12 satellite to help fast-track Digital India
Deepak Mathur, Executive Vice-President, Global Sales at SES Video, said that, the successful launch of SES-12 satellite recently will help India’s direct-to-home (DTH) TV market.

Successful launch of SES-12 to help fast-track Digital India:
i. Deepak Mathur said that, in line with the Telecom Regulatory Authority of India’s (TRAI) recent directive to allow mobile and Internet services in Indian airspace, the satellite’s concentrated beams could also offer cost-effective capacity to enable inflight connectivity services.

World Sports Journalists Day – July 2
On 2nd July 2018, World Sports Journalists Day was celebrated all over the world.

World Sports Journalists Day:
i. World Sports Journalists Day is celebrated on July 2 every year to honour the services of sports journalists towards the promotion of sports.
ii. World Sports Journalists Day was established by the International Sports Press Association (AIPS) in 1994 to mark the 70th anniversary of its foundation.
iii. The International Sports and Press Association (AIPS) was formed in 1924 during the Paris Summer Olympics.
