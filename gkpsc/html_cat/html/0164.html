Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker

  • Born on: 11 January 1825
  • Born at: Karthikapally Taluk (Alappuzha)
  • Original name: Kalisseri Velayudha Panicker
  • House Name: Kalisseri Tharavadu
  • Father: Kalisseri Perumal Chekor
  • Wife: Velumpy
  • Who gave him the title "Panicken" (later Panicken was modified as Panicker)
  • Travancore King
  • Who organised "ACHIPUDAVA STRIKE" in Kayamkulam is Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker
  • (Achipudava is a Cloth or Mundu that Covers the portion below the knee)
  • "MOOKKUTHY CHANTHA" Incidence in Pandalam was related to Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker
  • (Purpose = Lower class women to wear gold ornaments)
  • The first temple for Avarnas installed by an Avarna in Kerala
  • Mangalam Temple
  • The founder of Edackadu Njaneshwaram Temple (1854)
  • Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker
  • Edackadu Njaneshwaram Temple is located in
  • Mangalam Village (Alappuzha)
  • The idol of lord siva was installed by
  • Viswanathan Gurukkal of Kandiyoor
  • Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker built another temple in
  • Cheruvaranam (1855)
  • The founder of Kalisseri Kathakali Yogam
  • Answer : Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker
  • Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker Started Kalisseri Kathakali Yogam with the help of
  • Tharananaloor Namboothiri
  • The teachers of Kathakali Yogam
  • Mritwe Pattar and Ambalapuzha Madhava Kurup
  • Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker was Killed in
  • 1874
  • (During a boat journey in Kayamkulam Kayal by a group of upper class people who attacked him from behind)
  • Community Hall in memory of Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker is situated in
  • Mangalam (Alappuzha)
  • Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker Research Foundation and Cultural Centre is situated at
  • Mangalam Village (Alappuzha)
  • SMARANIKA - 2005 meeting held on
  • 10 April 2005
  • (Conducted = Arattupuzha Velayudha Panicker Research Foundation and Cultural Centre)
