National News

1. Delhi hosts 8th Theatre Olympics at Red Fort

  • The 51-day international theatre festival having 450 shows will be held over 17 cities including Chandigarh, Bengaluru and Mumbai.
  • The theme of the event is ‘Flag of Friendship’.

2. Rahul Gandhi calls for 40% reservation for youth in Congress

  • Congress already has 33% reservation for women and 20% for SC.
  • The 40% quota for youth would be over and above this.

3. Air Odisha launches its first flight

  • Gujarat CM Vijay Rupani launched the maiden flight of Bhubaneshwar based airline connecting Ahmedabad and Mundra.
  • The airline has been established under the Centre’s UDAN scheme.

International News

1. All 66 onboard killed in Iran plane crash

  • All 66 people on an Aseman Airlines ATR plane have been killed after the aircraft crashed in Iran’s southern Isfahan province.
  • The aircraft crashed in a mountainous area near Semirom, a remote town 620 km south of the capital Tehran.

2. No terror camps on our soil : Pak Army Chief

  • Pakistan’s Army Chief General Qamar Javed Bajwa has said that there are no terror camps in the country although the presence of terrorists cannot be ruled out.
  • He added that Pakistan hosts nearly 27 lakh Afghan refugees.

3. Why does US have veto rights and not India, asks Iran

  • Iranian President Hassan Rouhani questioned why India with a population of over 1 billion does not have veto power at the UNSC.
  • He further said that giving veto rights to the 5 nuclear nations at the time of formation was unfair.

Business News

1. 200 shell firms, benami assets under scanner in PNB scam

  • The ED has seized diamonds, gold and jewellery worth Rs.5,674 cr till now.
  • The accused are suspected to have used the shell firms to launder money and create benami assets.

2. Fairfax to buy 51% of Catholic Syrian Bank

  • The deal will value CSB at over Rs.1200 cr.
  • It will be the first takeover of an Indian bank by a foreign non-banking financial entity after the RBI modified ownership rules in May last year.

3. City Union Bank hit by Rs.12 cr fraud

  • No entries were made in the bank’s ledgers and transfers were pushed through SWIFT messaging platform.
  • The lender said it was able to block a remittance of $500,000 immediately which was made to a Dubai-based bank from Standard Chartered in New York.

2. India beat SA to register 5th straight T20I win

  • India registered their 5th consecutive victory in T20Is after defeating SA by 28 runs in the first T20I at Johannesberg.
  • The 2nd T20I of the 3 match series will take place on 21st February.
