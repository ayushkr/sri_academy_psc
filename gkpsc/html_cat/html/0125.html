--------- Supreme Court of India--------

Article 124 provides for the establishment and constitution of the Supreme Court

Supreme Court of India was inaugurated on 28th January 1950

During the British Rule the Supreme Court was known as the "Federal Court of India", which was established under the Govt.of India Act 1935

Supreme Court is called as the "Watch dog of the Constitution of India"

At present there is a Chief Justice and 30 Judges in the Supreme Court

The Chief Justice and Judges of the Supreme Court are appointed by the President

The senior most Judge of the Supreme Court is appointed as the Chief Justice of India

The first Chief Justice of India was H.J.Kania.

Longest serving Chief Justice of India is Y.V. Chandrachood

Shortest serving Chief Justice- Kamal Narayan Singh (17 days)

The first Malayalee to become a judge of Supreme Court is Justice P. Govinda Menon

The first woman Judge of the Supreme Court is Fathima Beevi

The first Malayalee woman Judge is also Fathima Beevi

The first Malayalee to become the Chief Justice of India is K.G.Balakrishnan

Current Chief Justice of India is Justice Dipak Mishra.
(He is the 45th Chief Justice of India)

Delhi is the permanent seat of the Supreme Court

The oath of affirmation to the Judges of the Supreme Court is administrated by the President

A Judge of the Supreme Court holds office till he attains the age of 65 years

Article 143 of the Constitution deals with the power of President to consult Supreme Court

The PIN Code of Supreme Court is 110201

The Supreme Court is known as the Court of Record (under Article 129)

The main block of the Supreme Court building has been designed in an 'Indo- British' style by the chief architect Ganesh Bhikaji Deolalikar

Ganesh Bhikaji Deolalikar was the first Indian to head the Central Public Works Department

Mother and Child Sculpture in the foreground of the Supreme Court building was made by the renowned artist Chintamoni Kar
