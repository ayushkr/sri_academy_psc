Scalars and Vectors
Scalar is a quantity that has only magnitude Examples:

  1. • distance
  2. • time
  3. • mass
  4. • speed
  5. • area
  6. • work
  7. • energy
  8. • pressure

Vector is a quantity that has magnitude and direction

Examples:

  1. • displacement
  2. • velocity
  3. • acceleration
  4. • force
  5. • momentum
  6. • electric field strength
