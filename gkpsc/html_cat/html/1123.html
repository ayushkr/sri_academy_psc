Kerala Police Physical Test Details

The Kerala Police 2018 Physical Test will be calculated for both men and women. But the ranges may get differed. Kerala Police 2018 Physical Test Recruitment also available in the official websites. The candidates should complete the certain test within given records. They are mentioned as below.

For Men:

S.No Test Duration
1 100 meter run 14 seconds
2 High jump 132.20cm(4’6”)
3 Long jump 457.20cm(15’)
4 Putting the shot (7264 grams) 609.60 cms (20’)
5 Throwing the cricket ball 6096 cm (200’)
6 Rope climbing (only with hand) 365.80cm(12’)
7 Pulls up or chinning 8 minutes
8 1500 meter run 5 minutes 44 seconds

For Women:

S.No Test Duration
1 100 meter run 17 seconds
2 High jump 1.06 meters
3 Long jump 3.05 meters
4 Putting the shot (4 kg) 4.88 meters
5 Throwing the thrown ball 14 meters
6 Shuttle race (25*4 meters) 26 seconds
7 Skipping (one minute) 80 times
