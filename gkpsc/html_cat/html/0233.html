National News

1. Congress releases ‘chargesheet’ against AAP on govt’s 3 years

  • Created in the form of a booklet, the chargesheet includes an evaluation of different departments of Delhi.
  • It also carries a comparison of AAP’s performance with the previous Sheila Dikshit-led Congress govt.

2. Maha farmers lose crops to hailstorm

  • The state govt has sought Rs.200 cr aid from the Centre after farmers lost around 2 lakh hectares of land.
  • Farmers are entitled to Rs.6800 per hectare up to 2 hectares.

3. Railways initiates recruitment process for 89,000 employees

  • The Indian Railways has initiated the recruitment process for hiring 89,000 Group C and D employees.
  • The additional recruitment will cost around Rs.3000 cr.

International News

1. Dutch law makes all adults organ donors unless they opt out

  • The law will come into effect in 2020 and ask the citizens for their consent for being a donor.
  • Those who will fail to reply twice will be automatically made the donors.

2. Maldives warns against Indian intervention in its crisis

  • Maldives’ Defence Minister Adam Umar has warned that any talk of Indian military intervention in his country’s ongoing political crisis could affect Maldives-India relations.
  • Maldives’ ex-President Mohamed Nasheed had sought India’s help in resolving the crisis.

3. Jacob Zuma Resigns as South Africa’s President

  • Jacob Zuma, South Africa’s embattled and controversial president, announced his resignation in a televised address to the nation. He faced a no-confidence vote in the Parliament.
  • Cyril Ramaphosa has been elected as the President.

Business News

1. PNB has capacity to recover from $1.77bn fraud: CEO

  • PNB’s CEO Sunil Mehta said the bank will honour all the commitments if liability falls on them.
  • Foreign branches of Indian banks had loaned money to Nirav Modi-linked firms based on fake guarantees provided by PNB.

2. No GST on food served to patients admitted to hospital

  • The food served by the hospital to patients who are not admitted will be taxable.
  • Also, there will be no GST on services provided by doctors, consultants and technicians at the hospital.

3. Air India to retain brand name after privatisation : Reports

  • Reports claimed that the govt plans to lay down a condition to prospective bidders that the airline’s name should be retained after the disinvestment process.
  • The govt is also considering selling between 51% and 76% stake in the airline.

  • As many as 16 teams will participate in the event.

2. Ronaldo 1st ever player with 100 goals for one club in UCL

  • Cristiano Ronaldo’s overall tally has gone up to 116 goals, 19 more than Lionel Messi.
  • Ronaldo has scored 10+ UCL goals for the 7th straight season.
