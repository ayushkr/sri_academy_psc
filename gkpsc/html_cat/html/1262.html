Pakyong Airport - 24 - 9 - 2018

Pakyong Airport is a greenfield airport near Gangtok, the state capital of Sikkim, India. The airport, spread over 400 ha, is located at Pakyong village about 35km south of Gangtok. At 4500 ft, Pakyong Airport is one of the five highest airports in India.
