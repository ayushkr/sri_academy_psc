Articles Basic Rule

'a' is used before words beginning with a consonant sound - boom.

eg: A pen, A student, A bag

'an' is used before a vowel sound

eg. An umbrella, An American, An interesting story

"A' is added to the words which begin with ‘у’ sound and ‘w’ sound.

eg. a European, a uniform, a union(y sounded words), a one rupee note, a one way ticket, one legged sailor(w sounded Words)

"An” is used before abbreviations pronounced with a vowel sound at the beginning.

eg. My friend is an M.P.

Don't use 'the’ before the names of games

games-ന്റെ മുൻപിൽ 'the’ ചേർക്കരുത്

eg: football, baseball,cricket etc

"The should not be used before the names of an substances if they are used in a general sense.
